Women's Sequins Thread Embroidered Georgette Lehenga Choli

Women's Sequins Thread Embroidered Georgette Lehenga Choli. Sequins Thread Embroidered: The lehenga choli features sequin work and thread embroidery, suggesting it's embellished and likely has intricate detailing.Georgette Fabric: Georgette is a lightweight, flowing fabric commonly used in Indian ethnic wear. It's known for its softness and drape.The lehenga is a long, flared skirt that usually reaches the ankles. It's typically heavily embellished with embroidery, sequins, beads, or other decorative elements. Lehengas come in various styles, including A-line, fishtail, and flared, and they are known for their intricate designs and vibrant colors.
